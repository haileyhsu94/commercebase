# CommerceBase — How It Works

> A non-technical explanation of what happens when an advertiser uses CommerceBase.

---

## What the advertiser does

The advertiser only makes four decisions:

1. **Goal** — "I want to drive sales" (or: new customers, target ROAS, brand awareness)
2. **Budget** — "€5,000 per day"
3. **Products** — "My SS26 Sneakers collection"
4. **Market** — "Europe"

They hit Launch. That's it.

---

## What CommerceBase does automatically

### Step 1: AI splits the budget across channels

CommerceBase looks at the goal and decides where the money goes:

> "Sales is the goal — so I'll put most of the budget where people are already looking to buy."

The budget gets distributed across our network. The advertiser doesn't see or choose which channels — they just see results.

### Step 2: Ads go live across the entire network

**Google & Bing Shopping** — When someone searches "nike air max 90," the advertiser's product appears as a shopping ad. These people are already looking to buy, so conversion rates are highest here.

**Our shopping sites (Realry, Sneakers123, FlexDog)** — When someone browses sneakers on our sites, the advertiser's products get priority placement at the top.

**500+ fashion websites (DailyClicks)** — When someone reads a fashion article on Highsnobiety, Hypebeast, or any of 500+ partner sites, they see the advertiser's products as native ads.

**2,400+ creators (StylMatch)** — Fashion creators on Instagram and TikTok get an offer to feature the products. They create authentic content and earn commission on sales.

### Step 3: People buy

Here is the key difference from other ad platforms:

- **Other platforms** know someone clicked. They *estimate* whether a purchase happened.
- **CommerceBase** knows someone bought. We own the shopping infrastructure, so **actual order confirmations** come directly into our system. Not estimates — confirmed sales.

### Step 4: AI learns every night

Every night, the AI looks at the day's actual purchase data:

> "Google Shopping sold €18,000 worth. Our shopping sites sold €10,000. Web ads only sold €3,800 — let me shift some budget from web ads to Shopping tomorrow."

This adjustment happens automatically, every day. The advertiser does nothing.

### Step 5: It gets smarter over time

This is the most important part.

Every campaign that runs on CommerceBase generates purchase data. That data teaches the AI which products sell best, through which channels, to which types of shoppers.

The more campaigns run → the more purchase data → the smarter the AI → the better the results → the more advertisers join → repeat.

Other ad platforms learn from clicks. We learn from **actual sales**. Over time, this advantage compounds.

## For existing partners (affiliates)

Many of our 3,600+ merchants already work with us through affiliate partnerships — they pay commission when a sale happens, and their products already appear on Google Shopping and our sites.

**CommerceBase doesn't replace this.** It adds on top:

| What exists today | What CommerceBase adds |
|---|---|
| Products on Google Shopping (affiliate) | Smarter bidding by AI — same cost, more sales |
| Products on Realry/Sneakers123 (organic) | **[Sponsored]** priority slots — paid, additional visibility |
| Nothing on 500+ web publishers | **DailyClicks** — entirely new exposure channel |
| Nothing with creators | **StylMatch** — creator content driving sales |

Existing affiliate commission structure stays exactly the same. CommerceBase only charges for the new, additional exposure.

**The pitch to existing partners:**
> "Everything you have keeps working. CommerceBase gives you two things on top: more reach (500+ websites, 2,400+ creators) and smarter optimization (AI that learns from your actual sales). You only pay for the extra."

---

## For new partners

New partners sign up, connect their product catalog (Shopify, Cafe24, etc.), register a credit card, and create their first campaign.

Their products start appearing across the entire CommerceBase network immediately. They pay per click (CPC) for all channels. No affiliate complexity — simple, direct relationship.

**Advertisers set a goal and budget. CommerceBase AI decides where, when, and how to spend it across the entire open internet — and learns from every confirmed purchase to get better every day.**
